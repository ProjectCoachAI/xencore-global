Our story

Twenty years of enterprise expertise — now building what's next.

Headquartered in Wollerau, Switzerland. Operating globally. Built on Infor. Expanding into AI and environmental technology.

Xencore Global AG was founded on a single conviction: enterprise technology should work harder for the businesses that depend on it. For two decades we built that conviction into one of Europe's most experienced Infor ERP delivery organisations — implementing, migrating, integrating, and supporting Infor LN, M3, and CloudSuite for manufacturers, distributors, and service organisations across the continent.

That foundation didn't just give us a client base. It gave us a front-row seat to how businesses actually make decisions — and how slow, fragmented, and costly that process had become. Forge was our answer. An AI decision platform that fires one question simultaneously at eight leading AI engines and returns perspectives, synthesis, and a consensus score in under 30 seconds. Built by Xencore. Used by professionals every day.

Our third frontier is the planet. When a prominent Swiss client needed technology to manage and certify reforestation at scale, we built a platform that handles field polygon management, satellite monitoring, carbon credit calculation, and Verra-standard reporting. Technology that turns environmental intent into verifiable, auditable impact.

We are a small team with a long track record and a broad technology capability. We don't do everything. We do three things exceptionally well — and we do them from Switzerland, where precision is not optional.
