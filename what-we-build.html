Forge — the decision
layer on top of AI.

Forge is our flagship AI product, built by Xencore Global and used by professionals daily. Ask one question — eight AI engines answer simultaneously. The strongest answer surfaces instantly, backed by a consensus score across all responses. A decision engine, not just a chatbot.
Broadcast
Fire one question to all 8 AIs simultaneously. The strongest answer surfaces instantly — no side-by-side review required.
Perspectives
See all AI responses side by side with a live consensus score. Streamed in real time — ~30 seconds from question to synthesis.
Synthesis
Forge reads every AI response and produces one Best-of-Best answer. Multiple analysis modes: Executive Summary, Consensus Map, Divergence Spotlight.
Excel Analysis
Drop any .xlsx, .csv, or .tsv file. Ask a question. Eight AIs analyse your data simultaneously with automatic write-back fixes.
AI Diary
Every decision logged and searchable. Add a note to each synthesis: "What did you decide?" — the only AI product that closes the loop.
Forge Dock
Chrome extension that sits on every AI you already use. Switch providers, get perspectives, and access Forge without leaving the page.

Environmental Technology · Swiss client

Reforestation technology
that certifies
real impact.

Built for a prominent Swiss environmental client, our reforestation platform manages the full lifecycle of carbon credit certification — from field polygon delineation and satellite monitoring to Verra-standard verification and reporting. Technology that turns reforestation into verifiable, tradeable impact.
Built for Verra certification standards. Our platform manages field polygon data, automated monitoring reports, carbon sequestration calculations, and chain-of-custody documentation — giving environmental organisations a defensible, auditable record of every hectare restored. Deployed for a leading Swiss client operating reforestation projects across multiple geographies.
Request briefing
Field polygon management
Precise delineation and tracking of reforestation zones. GPS-verified boundaries with audit-ready coordinate records.
Satellite monitoring
Continuous remote sensing integration for forest cover change detection, canopy density measurement, and anomaly alerting.
Verra-standard reporting
Automated generation of VCS/REDD+ compliant documentation for submission to Verra certification bodies.
Carbon credit calculation
Transparent, auditable carbon sequestration modelling with species-specific growth curves and soil carbon accounting.
Chain of custody
End-to-end audit trail from land preparation through certification, credit issuance, and retirement — fully traceable.
Dashboard & reporting
Real-time project dashboards for landowners, investors, and certifiers — exportable to PDF, CSV, and regulatory formats.
